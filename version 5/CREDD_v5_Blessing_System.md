# Blessing System

Every deity possesses a unique **Divine Blessing**, representing the true manifestation of their divine authority.

Additional deity slots grant partial stats and allow access to an **Echo Blessing**, a weakened manifestation inherited from an allied deity.

---

# Party Rules

## Slot 1

- Grants **100% of the deity's stats**.
- Activates the deity's **Divine Blessing**.
- Only one Divine Blessing can ever be active.

---

## Slot 2

- Grants **50% of the deity's base stats**.
- Does **not** activate its blessing.

---

## Slot 3

- Grants **50% of the deity's base stats**.
- Unlocks the **Echo Blessing** system.
- Choose **one eligible Echo Blessing** from either the Slot 2 or Slot 3 deity.
- Only one Echo Blessing may be equipped.

This means a full party has:

- 1 Divine Blessing
- 1 Echo Blessing
- 100% stats from Slot 1
- 50% stats from Slot 2
- 50% stats from Slot 3

# Divine Resonance

Equipping specific combinations of deities grants additional base stat bonuses through **Divine Resonance**.

These bonuses are always active and do not affect Divine Blessings or Echo Blessings.
